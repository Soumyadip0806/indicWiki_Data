songe kate herel tae songe nepelok’ mente kad orak’ teye senena. Apat relte
gidra kin do uni then hewok’ lagit’ aditet’ kin chat-pataoena. Menkhan auriyak'ge
ente apat ar gidra kin talare tah& kan mérhét’ reak’ silpirt do bako jhic'atkoa,
Gidra kin do apat tet’ barhe sec‘odokok’ lagit’ rak’ rak’ tekin nehor adea. Gidra
kinak’ nonkan obosta ffel te ketec’ monan Kushal Kowarak’ jiwi ontor do bhabna
te perec’en taea, Met’ dak’ atuen taea. Kushal Kowar ren dhorom juri Probhabati
Kowar do birbanta herelak’ andak’ tawak’ nel kate hereltaye metadea, “Cedakem
bhabna'k kana? Alom bhabnak’a, Nelme unak’ hor tala khon Thakur do amgeye
bachao akat'mea. Bhagan geam.”
