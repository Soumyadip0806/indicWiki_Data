Gel are sae mit’ sal re Kushal Kowar do Golaghat Mahakuma reak’
mit’ten adi bhitri ato reye janamlen tah@kana. Uni oka ato reye janamlen ona
atore bijimarsal ban tahékana, akil reak’ marsal hd onka leka ona atore bari
seter akan tahe kana, Moto gadi se khobor kagoc ho ona atoren horko bako nel
akawan tahe kana. Noate ona ato reak’ obosta do abo sanam kobon bujhau
dareak’ kana,

Kushal Kowar katic’ korae tahé kan rege Ifgraji Iskul re futum ko aderlet’
taea. Dher casa horkoren iskul gidra koak’, ona oktere cet’ obosta hoyok’ kan
tahé kana, Kushal Kowarak’ obosta hd enka ge hoyen taea. Orak’ reak’ kami
aujharte parhao do bagi hoyen taea. Iskule bagi ket’ khange uni do asulok’ reak’
dand paraoadea. Uni do mit’ ten hopon dokane khulau leda, menkhan ona dokan
hO aema din ba? tikau len taea. Ina kate Kushal Kowar do cabagan re kami
sendraye ehop'keda. Barea pea cabagan re uni do kerani muhuri eman reak’e
kamileda. Menkhan oka jaega rege uni do kamire or bae bujhauleda, uniak’ ontor
do betabege tahé kan taea ar ona iatege disom bidisia koak’ tabe re tahé kante
jaoge uni do okalekate disomBetabe hoe dareak’ ona reak’e hudiset’ tah kana.
Onka lekare cakri bagi kate uni do orak’ teye ruarena. Ca-bagan reye kami kan
tahé kan rege Kushal Kowar do Probhabati Kowar nutumanic’ kusiye bapla ledea
Cakri khon ruar kate uni do casa kami kate asulok’e ehop leda. Horupothar totha
reye berelente casa kami re mone lagao keda
