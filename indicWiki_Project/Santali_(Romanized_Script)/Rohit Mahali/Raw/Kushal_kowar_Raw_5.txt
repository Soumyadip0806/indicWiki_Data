bhag kana. Onate indon! bhagan gea. If do Mohatma Gandhiak’ katha leka dular
ar ‘sariak’ reak’ in’ porcar akada. Raska jonpe nulasok’ pe, ente aboak’ jonom
disom do mit’ din ban mit’ din betabek’ getabona...

Dosar hilok’ adi paha pahore Kushal Kowar do ace tahe kan kandha bahre
pindare hor taram sadeye anjomkeda. Mit’ ghari tayom khange sipahi kodo duar
ko jhic’ keda. Uni doye kuligot’ket’ koa, “ Okte seteren tina se cet'?” Onde khon
sipahi kodo phasi thai then uni ko idi kedea. Phasi ko horok’ade sorige te uni
doye menkeda, “E Ram parom kan me.” Ina ror mucad tege sanamak’ cabaena.

Jorhat kad orak’ reak’ phasi kat re Kushal Kowar do jiwiye alae keda.
Menkhan birbanta Kushal Kowarak’ nutum jaejug dhabic’ sanam koak’ monre
tahé akae tahena.
