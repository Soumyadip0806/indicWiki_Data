Thora din tayom khange Mohotma Gandhiak’ ayur te namdak’ “Bulun an
bebatao laraona” ehop’ena, ona do ehop' lena 1930 salre. Noa laraona ehop’en
khan ge Kushal Kowar do ona laraona re fir khadlek’ lagit’ at hoyen taya. Gota
mon ar jiwi te uni do ona laraonae sohot'ada. Noa laraona ehop'en khange
Kushal Kowar do bulun jom doye bagileda, 1942 sal re uni do kad orak’reko
bhorao ledea. Noa ghotna khonge uniak’ mon reak’ ketec'tet’ ar laraona re mone
lagao reak’ numuna sodorok’ kantaea.

Kushal Kowar do gel bar serma Bharot reak’ jatiari korigres dolreye kami
leda. Uni do olok’ parhaok’ re komgeye tahé kan reho, Mohotma Gandhiak’ ayur
te hoelen betabe laraona reak’ rehet’ katha agi ate bujhau orom leda. Nui do
mitten gakhur ak’yurice tah@ kana. Horupothar tothare korigres dol reak’ dare do
agi atge barti len tahé kana

Pongel bar sal reak’ laraona do gota Bharot re serigel leka pasnacena
Thik inako okte rege Japan ren larhai phad kodo Bharot reak’ purub nakha sec're
jhap ko emkeda. British sipahi ko do onko tengo daram ko lagit’ ko tefigo takena
Ina tak rege Bharot disom betabe lagit’ ko odoklen hulgaria ko do British
sarkarak’ rel sorok ko raput’ keda, relgadi ko ultau keda, sarkari orak’ kore sefigel
ko jundiada ar aema sarkari sompatti ko nosto gidi keda.
